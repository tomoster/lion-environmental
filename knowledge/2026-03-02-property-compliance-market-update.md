## Headline: LL87 Deadline Is Today — March 31 Filing Window Now Active

NYC DOB extended the Local Law 87 Energy Efficiency Report (EER) deadline from 12/31/2025 to **March 31, 2026**. This is for the 2025 filing year only. Buildings that miss the March 31 deadline face:
- **$3,000** civil penalty for first year of non-compliance
- **$5,000** for each subsequent year

This does not change future LL87 cycles — 2026+ filings revert to December 31 deadlines.

**Source:** https://prisenyc.com/nyc-extends-local-law-87-deadline-to-march-2026-what-it-means-for-building-owners

## LL97 Carbon Compliance — 2026 Enforcement Reality

**94% of covered NYC buildings** have either filed required LL97 documents, requested extensions, or are in discussions with NYC DOB. The remaining 6% face immediate penalty exposure.

Key 2026 enforcement facts:
- Penalties: **$268/metric ton CO₂e** over the annual limit
- "Good faith effort" grace period is over — 2026 is when penalties become real financial events
- Buildings with ≥35% rent-regulated units now covered starting 2026 (November 2020 amendment)
- First LL97 submission for newly-covered residential buildings: **May 1, 2027**
- Asset values already being discounted by buyers to account for emissions fines

**Source:** https://www.enr.com/articles/61653-most-nyc-buildings-have-begun-local-law-97-emissions-compliance

## LL84 Benchmarking — May 1, 2026 Deadline Approaching

The Local Law 84 benchmarking deadline for 2026 (covering CY2025 data) is **May 1, 2026**. Non-compliance penalties: **$500/quarter** until compliant. Energy efficiency grades must be posted near each public entrance; failure to post: **$1,250/year**.

**Source:** https://energo.com/blog/nyc-local-law-84-2026-deadline-penalties-how-to-stay-compliant/
