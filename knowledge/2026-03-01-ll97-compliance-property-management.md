**LL97 enforcement landscape (current state — published ~2 weeks ago):**
- NYC DOB enforcing $268/metric ton CO₂e over the allowed annual limit
- 2026 is a critical inflection year: regulators expect owners to move beyond short-term fixes toward long-term retrofit strategies
- Buildings >25,000 sq ft covered; some under Article 320 starting 2024, 2026, or 2035 depending on classification
- Example fine: 75 tons over limit = $20,100/year — recurring until compliant
- Common exemptions: houses of worship, some rent-regulated residential, city-owned buildings (still may require benchmarking documentation)
- LL97 works in tandem with Local Law 84 (benchmarking), Local Law 88 (lighting/sub-metering)
